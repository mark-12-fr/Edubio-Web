Help & Support

Dashboard / Help

Need Assistance?

Check the Frequently Asked Questions below or contact the IT Support Desk for further assistance.

I Can't Log In

Ensure your fingerprint is already registered at the designated IT station and that your full name matches your official school records.

Fingerprint Not Recognized

Make sure your finger is clean and dry before scanning. If the issue persists, visit the IT Office to update your biometric profile.

Forgot Password

Use the Forgot Password option on the login page or coordinate with the System Administrator to verify your identity.
