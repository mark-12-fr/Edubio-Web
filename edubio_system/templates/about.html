About the System

Dashboard / About

System Overview

The PHINMA UI Attendance Portal is a biometric-based system that uses fingerprint scanning to accurately record student and faculty attendance. It provides secure, reliable, and tamper-resistant attendance tracking for the institution.

Our Mission

Our mission is to reduce manual paperwork and streamline the attendance process, allowing students and teachers to focus more on learning, productivity, and classroom engagement.

How It Works

After registration, the user's fingerprint is enrolled into the system. During attendance, the fingerprint is scanned and verified against the database to grant secure access and log attendance automatically.

Data Privacy

We prioritize your privacy and security. Fingerprint data is converted into encrypted biometric templates and is never stored as raw images, ensuring maximum protection of user data.
